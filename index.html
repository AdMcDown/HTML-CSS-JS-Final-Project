PRO GAMER GLASSES

The Pro-Gamer glasses are glasses that allow the user to see the world in a new way!

Gaming

Try our games like Z-Apocalypse, fight waves of zombies out in the world, whether you play in a field, the woods, a busy highway(not recommended) or even a cemetery! once you start the game, zombies will pop up just about everywhere and they are coming for you!

Augmented Reality

Do you find your day to day scenery boring? well spice up your daily walk with some of our Augmented Reality features! download some creates to travel around your world such as DRAGONS flying around in the sky! or maybe you you want to see cool new plants! add some vegitation mods from your fav games like SKYRIM. WHO DOESNT LOVE THE ATMOSPHERE OF SKYRIM!!!!!

Programming

THIS still not good enough for you!? then how about you program your own features into your glasses! You can program the world around you to look how you want, don't like you neighbour? program in some bunny ears on his head any time you see him "HA". have a boring hill top in your area with nothing on it? Program a castle on top of that because WHY NOT! or maybe you have a new game you want to make!

Gear

You may need some gear to make all of this work, Don't worry we've got you covered! you can buy all the product right here in our shop. Head on over!

With the Programmer glasses its all possible and the worlds you create, you can upload online so others can enjoy it!
